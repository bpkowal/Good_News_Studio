Care Ethics Response:
In this situation, care ethics would prioritize empathy, responsiveness, and moral attention to the specific people involved. You should consider your neighbor's perspective and feelings. Since they have stated that the money is not theirs, it would be dishonest and disregarding of their autonomy to take it for yourself. Instead, you could offer to split the money with them or return it to them, depending on their preference and your relationship with them. In this way, care ethics values the relational closeness between neighbors and emphasizes responsiveness to the unique situation and emotional needs of those involved.
