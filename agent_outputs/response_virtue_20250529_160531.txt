From the perspective of virtue ethics, the decision of how to split the $50 between you and your friend should be guided by the virtues and the development of your moral character. The virtues that come into play here are generosity, fairness, and friendship.

Fairness, on the other hand, calls us to treat everyone equally, to distribute benefits and burdens according to what is deserved or equitable. Friendship is the bond that connects us to others, creating a sense of closeness, trust, and commitment.
